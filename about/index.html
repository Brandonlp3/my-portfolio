About Me¶

I'm Brandon Parker — BSCS (AI + HCI) and incoming MSCS (Machine Learning) student at Georgia Tech. I work where cloud infrastructure, full‑stack product engineering, and applied ML meet human-centered design.

Core Principles¶

  1. Ship iteratively—optimize feedback loops early.
  2. Make performance & reliability observable by design.
  3. Simplicity scales; abstractions must earn their keep.
  4. AI augments workflows when context is preserved.
  5. Documentation should explain intent, not just behavior.
